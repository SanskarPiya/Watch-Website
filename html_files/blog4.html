Smart Watch vs Traditional Watch

Jan 12, 2025

In recent few years after the surge of popularity of smart watches there has been a debate between consumers of smart watches and consumer of traditional watches on which type of watch is better. Both types of watches have their own pros and cons. We can compare these two in following categories:

  1. Functionality
    Smart watches are meant to be full of features that make the users life easier. Features such as syncing with your phone, GPS, sleep monitor, etc. Traditional watches mean while performing their simple task of telling time only.
  2. Durability and water resistance
    In the context of durability and water resistance, traditional watches are far superior to that of smartwatches. There mechanical body can take more punishment than smartwatches and their electronic components.
  3. Craftmanship and elegance
    Again, traditional watches triumph over smartwatches in terms of their craftmanship. Traditional watch making is an art with long history especially luxury watches.
  4. Cost
    The price range of traditional watches is a large range. You can buy one for 10$ and then buy another one for 100,000$. Whereas smartwatches are found in the range of 200$-700$.


In the end, it's ones preference for which type of watch they want. If one only wants a watch and not extra features, traditional watch will be the best option. If one needs to track their steps or their exercise then smartwatches are the better option. Ultimately, it's their lifestyle that determines the watch best for them.
