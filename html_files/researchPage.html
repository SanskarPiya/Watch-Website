Page

1st Comparision - Product Display In Home Page.
We were inspired by how the new arrival section was displayed on the home page of Watches.com. Its simplistic yet appealing and user-friendly design aligned with our website design philosophy. So, we implemented it in our home webpage, but with a few tweaks. Instead of displaying new arrivals, we used it to highlight and showcase our most loved watches.

2nd Comparision - Blog Display Page.
We took the reference for blog page from the blog page of Samaya Watch. While their blog page only displayed the blog's topics, we added a little below our blog's topics, not only to give it a clean and simple look but also to take a more reader-centric approach. This allows them to make a more informed decision on whether they want to continue reading, improving the page's effectiveness.

3rd Comparision - Blog Content Page.
Since, we had been seeing news a lot when using our devices, we thought why not do our blog page like a news page. So, we took the reference for blog page from NBC NEWS. We really liked how the other read recommendations were at the right hand side of the page while reading the news so, we decided to go with a similar concept and display other topics so that the reader doesn't have to go back to the blog page and instead could just click on the topic they are interested in to read the content.

4th Comparision - Product Details Page.
We really liked how products name and their details were displayed in Amazon shopping website. Along with its appealing visuals, its popularity has helped make this style of product detail page very familiar to consumers. So, we implemented it with little changes in our website for its appeal and familiarity.

5th Comparision - About Us Member Display Page.
We took the reference for About Us page from the leaderships of Riot Games. We really liked how the leaders of Riot Games were displayed on the webpage so, we also wanted to implement it. As you can see we changed our webpage a little bit from the origional webpage. The original webpage has the leaders position as well as a little bit description of what they do but in ours, we have only displayed the names of the members. We have also given a background color unlike the original webpage.

6th Comparision - Trust since Panel.
The "Trust Panel" from Jomashop caught our eyes as it was a perfect way to add credibility to the website, making it more trustworthy to any possible consumers. So we implemented their design with changes tailored to our website design philosophy. We added "Trust since 2006" and "10000+ Five Star Reviews" to convey the message of reliability and positive consumer experience that our website has been able to offer.

7th Comparision - Footer Section.
For the footer, we look for inspiration from Islington College'swebsite. We have a great experience on Islington's website due to the simple yet very straightforward design of the footer. It allows users to access all of the website features and provides contact details in a visually appealing presentation. So, we implemented the footer's idea and changes that seem more fitting for our website.
