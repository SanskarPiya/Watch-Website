Impact of Technology Advancement in Watch Industry

Jan 12, 2025

With the integration of modern technology, watch industry has evolved into a completely new industry with new practices, trends and consumer demands. It has had many positive impacts on industry along with many negative impacts. Some of them are listed below and explained in brief.

Positive impacts


  1. Technoloogy like 3d printing allows manufacturers to make intricate designs with materials which are hard to work with. The materials also make watch more durable and comfortable.
  2. With e-commerce, anyone can buy watches from any manufacturer from any part of the world increasing consumers for popular and niche watch manufacturers.
  3. Modern smart watches come with various functions like connecting with you phone, being able to make calls, track you fitness, sleep and heartbeat, use GPS for navigation, etc.
  4. Modern Modern technology has made it possible to make watches with unprecedented levels of accuracy. Technique like atomic clocks and quartz movements make sures watch time is precise.


Negative impacts


  1. Smartwatches software might get bugged or any new update might be incompatible with the hardware and cause software failure.
  2. Smartwatches and it's electronic components have shorter lifespan than traditional mechanical watches.
  3. Smartwatches collective sensitive data like your health, location and more. Along with that, smartwatches are connected to your phone. So, breach of data can lead to theft of very sensitive data.
  4. For the modern manufacturing process, machine that are far precise then human are used leading to machine being used for highly sensitive manufacturing work that were performed by skilled manpower.
